WhoPinnedIt?

How well do you really know your friends' Pinterests?

This is a Pinterest game where you guess which of your pinners pinned the pin. Currently it only works on a desktop computer:

  • Drag the link below into your browser's address bar like a bookmark.
  • Once you've done that, login to pinterest.com.
  • If you haven't already, switch to Pinterest's "New Look"
  • Then just click the 'WhoPinnedIt?' button you just made.
  • Happy guessing!
